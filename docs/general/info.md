
# 🌍 CityPulse: Predictive Urban Intelligence for Tunis

## 🧭 Overview

**CityPulse** is a data-driven project that aims to help us understand, improve, and predict how the city of Tunis functions for its citizens. Instead of just showing information, CityPulse uses powerful data science tools to **discover problems**, **reveal inequality**, and **help make smarter decisions**.

This is not just a data project—it’s a **city insight engine**, giving governments, citizens, and researchers the ability to see *where the city is failing*, *how people move and live*, and *what decisions will shape a better future*.

---

## 🎯 Purpose and Vision

> **“Make Tunis smarter, fairer, and more efficient—through the power of data.”**

CityPulse collects and analyzes diverse data about Tunis: public transport schedules, population demographics, city maps, and more. But rather than just creating reports, the real goal is to **use this data to answer questions like**:

* Are some neighborhoods being left behind?
* How can we improve public transportation reliability?
* Where should new services (schools, clinics, bus stops) be added?
* What will the city need five years from now?

---

## 🧠 Key Impact Areas

CityPulse focuses on **five high-impact goals**, each centered on solving a real problem that people in Tunis face.

### 1. Improve Public Transportation Reliability

* Understand and predict when and where delays happen.
* Help transit agencies schedule better and avoid overcrowding.

### 2. Reveal Inequality in City Access

* Show which neighborhoods have poor access to schools, jobs, healthcare, or transport.
* Help city planners fix service gaps.

### 3. Simulate Life in the City

* Model the daily routines of people like students, workers, and the elderly.
* Use this to uncover what obstacles they face moving around.

### 4. Forecast Future Needs

* Predict how many people will need transportation or services in the future.
* Help avoid shortages before they happen.

### 5. Recommend Smarter Urban Planning

* Suggest where to build new metro lines or services.
* Use data to back every planning decision.

---

## 🌆 Why It Matters

Cities are complicated. But with the right data, we can:

* **Make them fairer** by helping everyone access services equally.
* **Make them smarter** by using predictions instead of guesses.
* **Make them ready for the future** by planning with confidence.

Tunis is growing—and CityPulse is here to help it grow the right way.

---

## 🗺️ City Focus

We focus on **Tunis**, Tunisia’s capital, because:

* It has both metro and bus systems.
* It faces real urban challenges.
* It has open data available on transportation and demographics.
* It represents many of the problems other growing cities face.

---

## 🤝 Who This Is For

CityPulse is useful to:

* **Urban planners**: to make informed decisions.
* **Local governments**: to understand where to invest.
* **Citizens**: to see how their city works—and where it doesn’t.
* **Researchers and students**: to explore complex urban issues with real data.
